About Us

With decades of experience across all sectors of construction, ClearPath Construction Management & Consulting brings proven expertise to every project. Our team has worked in nearly every role imaginable , as general contractors, owner’s representatives, project developers, and construction managers , on projects ranging from commercial and residential builds to large-scale renovations and ground-up developments.

We’ve owned and operated our own construction companies and proudly hold resumes that include work with Fortune Global 500 firms. This deep, hands-on experience allows us to understand construction from every angle , from the boardroom to the jobsite , and to deliver the knowledge, leadership, and accountability that our clients deserve.

While construction management is our core service, ClearPath maintains strong partnerships that extend beyond traditional management. Through our trusted network, clients can access expert support in project financing, marketing, design coordination, and more. But unlike many firms, our only financial interest lies in the services we provide directly. We do not profit from contractors, vendors, or any outside recommendations , our loyalty and focus remain 100% on you. We don’t play games. We deliver results.
