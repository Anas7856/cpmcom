FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What services does ClearPath provide?

ClearPath offers construction management, project development, consulting, and owner’s representation services. We oversee projects from initial planning through completion — ensuring budget control, quality assurance, and on-time delivery.

Do you work with both commercial and residential clients?

Yes. Our experience spans commercial, residential, and mixed-use developments. We adapt our management strategies to suit each project’s scale, budget, and technical requirements.

How is ClearPath different from other construction firms?

Unlike many firms, ClearPath has no financial interest in contractors or vendors. Our loyalty is solely to our clients — meaning every decision we make is based on your best interest, not outside partnerships.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines depend on scope, complexity, and permitting requirements. During our consultation, we create a tailored schedule and ensure every phase stays on track through proactive management and transparent reporting.

Can ClearPath assist with financing and design coordination?

Yes. Through our trusted partner network, clients gain access to financing support, architectural collaboration, and design coordination — all under the guidance of our experienced management team.
