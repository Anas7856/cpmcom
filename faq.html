What does ClearPath Construction Management & Consulting do?

ClearPath acts as your dedicated Owner’s Representative, managing every phase of your project with your best interests at the forefront. We oversee construction management, project development, and consulting services to ensure your project is delivered on time, within budget, and at the highest quality standards.

How is ClearPath different from contractors or builders?

Unlike contractors who profit from construction work, ClearPath works solely for you — the client. Our loyalty is not tied to any builder, vendor, or designer. We represent your best interests exclusively, ensuring transparency, accountability, and cost control at every stage of your project.

What types of projects do you manage?

We manage a wide range of projects including commercial developments, residential builds, large-scale renovations, and ground-up construction. Whether you’re planning a new facility or modernizing an existing space, our expertise ensures smooth execution from concept to completion.

At what stage should I bring ClearPath into my project?

The earlier, the better. We often begin during the pre-construction phase to help establish budgets, review designs, and coordinate with architects and engineers. Early involvement allows us to identify potential issues, optimize costs, and keep your project on a clear and efficient path from day one.

Do you provide cost management and scheduling services?

Absolutely. Cost control and scheduling are core to our management approach. We track budgets, analyze expenses, monitor timelines, and provide transparent reporting to ensure your project stays on schedule and within financial expectations.

Can ClearPath help if my project is already underway?

Yes — even if your project has already started, our team can step in to provide oversight, cost review, schedule assessment, and strategic consulting. We identify inefficiencies, resolve issues, and help get your project back on track with minimal disruption.

Do you work with clients outside of your region?

Yes. ClearPath serves clients nationwide, providing remote support and on-site project management across various states and regions. Whether local or long-distance, we tailor our services to meet your specific location and project requirements.

How do I get started with ClearPath?

Getting started is easy. Simply contact us through our website or email us directly. We’ll schedule a consultation to discuss your goals, challenges, and project scope — then create a tailored plan to protect your budget and maximize your project efficiency.
