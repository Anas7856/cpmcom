FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What services does ClearPath provide?

ClearPath offers comprehensive project development, construction management, and consulting services. We manage every stage — from feasibility and budgeting to construction and closeout — ensuring seamless coordination, cost certainty, and quality results on every project.

Do you work with both commercial and residential clients?

Absolutely. We serve both commercial and residential sectors, providing tailored management strategies for each. Whether it’s a complex mixed-use development or a high-end residence, our process adapts to your goals, budget, and technical needs.

How is ClearPath different from other construction firms?

ClearPath operates as a true owner’s representative — fully independent, with no ties to contractors or vendors. Our only commitment is to your project’s success, providing transparent guidance and decisions rooted in your best interest from start to finish.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary based on project scale, permitting, and complexity. We develop a customized schedule during initial planning and monitor progress closely — ensuring milestones are met, risks are mitigated, and completion stays aligned with expectations.

Can ClearPath assist with financing and design coordination?

Yes. ClearPath partners with trusted financial advisors, architects, and design professionals to streamline early development. From feasibility studies to design coordination, we ensure your project is fully aligned — financially and technically — before breaking ground.
